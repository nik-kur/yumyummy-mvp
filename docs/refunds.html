Refund Policy

Effective date: 28 February 2026  ·  Last updated: 18 March 2026

All payments are processed via Telegram Stars (XTR) directly inside the Telegram app. We do not use any external payment processor.

We want you to be completely satisfied with YumYummy. This Refund Policy explains when and how you may request a refund for your subscription.

1. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are a new subscriber and are not satisfied with the Service, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial subscription payment. This guarantee applies to:

  • Monthly subscription plans — within 14 days of the first payment;
  • Annual subscription plans — within 14 days of the first payment.

To qualify for the money-back guarantee, your request must be received within 14 calendar days of the original payment date. After this period, refunds are granted only at our discretion in exceptional circumstances (see Section 3).

2. How to Request a Refund

Contact us directly and we will process the refund on your behalf via Telegram's refund mechanism:

  • Email: nikikurb@gmail.com
  • Phone / WhatsApp: +971 50 828 9351

When contacting us, please include your Telegram username to help us locate your subscription quickly.

3. Refunds for Renewals & Exceptional Circumstances

Subscription renewal payments are generally non-refundable. However, we will consider refund requests on a case-by-case basis if:

  • The Service experienced prolonged downtime or a critical technical failure that prevented you from using it during the billing period;
  • A duplicate charge was made in error;
  • You were charged after a cancellation that was not processed correctly due to a technical error on our part.

We strongly encourage you to contact us directly to resolve any issues quickly and cooperatively.

4. Cancellation vs. Refund

Cancelling your subscription stops future charges but does not automatically generate a refund for the current billing period. If you wish to receive a refund for an amount already paid, you must submit a refund request as described above.

After cancellation, you will continue to have access to the Service until the end of the current billing period.

5. Subscription Cancellation Instructions

To cancel your subscription without requesting a refund:

  • Open Telegram Settings → My Stars → Subscriptions, find YumYummy, and cancel; or
  • Contact us at nikikurb@gmail.com and we will cancel it for you.

6. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed via Telegram Stars. The refunded Stars are returned to your Telegram Stars balance, typically within 1–3 business days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy at any time. We will notify you of material changes through the Service or by email. The updated policy applies to purchases made after the new effective date; purchases made before that date remain subject to the policy in effect at the time of purchase.
