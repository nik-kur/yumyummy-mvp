Billing & Subscription Issues

Payments are processed via Telegram Stars (XTR) inside the Telegram app or by bank card through Paddle.com, our merchant of record.

  • Telegram Stars subscriptions: manage or cancel via Telegram Settings → My Stars → Subscriptions.
  • Card subscriptions (Paddle): use the "Manage subscription" button in the bot's Profile section, or contact Paddle's buyer support via your order receipt email or at paddle.com/help.
  • You can always contact us directly at nikikurb@gmail.com and we will resolve the issue promptly.
