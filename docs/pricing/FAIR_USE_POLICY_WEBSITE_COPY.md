# Fair Use Policy Copy for yumyummy.ai

Use this text in Terms of Service and (optionally) in FAQ.

## Short Terms Clause

YumYummy provides generous usage within each plan. To keep service quality stable for all users, fair-use limits apply per billing period. In rare cases of exceptionally high usage, access may be temporarily limited. If this happens, we notify you in-product and you can contact support for help.

## FAQ Version

### Do you have unlimited usage?

YumYummy plans are designed for normal day-to-day nutrition tracking. We apply fair-use limits to protect service stability and quality. Most users never hit these limits.

### What happens if I hit a fair-use limit?

You will see an in-product notice with support contact details. Our team can review your case and help choose the best option.

## Compliance Notes

- Avoid using the word "unlimited" in pricing cards, ads, and paywall copy.
- Keep fair-use language consistent across:
  - Terms of Service
  - Paywall disclosures
  - Support templates
- Do not publish exact internal anti-abuse thresholds unless you are prepared to treat them as contractual limits.
